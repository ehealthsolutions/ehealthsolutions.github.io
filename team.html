Adam Tabriz M.D
Founder, CEO

Adam Tabriz M.D is the founder and CEO of both eHealth Solutions, Inc. and Virtumed360. He is an experienced leader for clinical, healthcare, biomedical and surgery centers for quality, patient safety, risk management, core measures, physician peer review, patient satisfaction and regulatory compliance. He brings his years of experience ensuring compliance with federal, state, local, AAAHC, JACHO and IMQ regulations and standards to the table. With his expertise in writing effective Healthcare business plans using effective market research and financial analyses, he can help any independent practice run efficiently and effectively.

Grant Koeneke
Co-founder, CTO

Grant Koeneke Grant is an IT Executive with more than 24 years of experience in the design and delivery of cost-effective, high-performance technology solutions in support of rapid growth with budget responsibilities in the tens of millions of dollars annually. Before coming to EHealth Solution, Grant has held executive positions and upper level management in various corporations including Microsoft, Intel and Hewlett Packard. He has a been part of large corporations as well as several startup companies. Most recently he held the title of CTO at startups Weedmaps and Klique. Grant is skilled in all phases of the project life cycle, from initial feasibility analysis and conceptual design through implementation and enhancement. He is also effective at building culturally diverse, team-centered operating units with excellent business process and strategy development skills. Grant’s background is in Database Technologies in the area of Administration, Design, Development, Data Warehousing and Business Intelligence. He continues to co-design systems as a Senior Developer focusing on the design and build out of complete end-to-end enterprise solutions.

Ali Taghavi

Ronald Ellsworth
